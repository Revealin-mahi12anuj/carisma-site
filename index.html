Education Hub

Understand what you eat.

Smarter carb choices start with better information.

What is Glycaemic Index (GI)?
GI measures how fast food raises blood sugar on a scale of 0–100. Below 55 is Low GI — slow, steady release. Regular potato scores 80. Carisma scores 52–58.
Why does GI matter for Indians?
India has 101 million diabetics — the highest in the world. Rice and potato are daily staples. Switching to lower GI versions of the same food is the most sustainable health intervention.
GI vs GL — what is the difference?
GI measures the speed of blood sugar rise. Glycaemic Load also accounts for how much you eat. Carisma has both a lower GI and 33% fewer carbs per serving — a double advantage.
What are Anthocyanins?
Anthocyanins are pigments that give purple foods their colour — powerful antioxidants, the same family found in blueberries and açaí. Carisma AOXY purple sweet potato is naturally rich in them.
What is Beta-Carotene?
Beta-Carotene converts to Vitamin A in the body, supporting vision, immune function, and skin health. Carisma VITA orange sweet potato is one of the richest available food sources.
Does cooking method affect GI?
Yes. Cooling cooked potato creates resistant starch, lowering GI further. Roasting is better than frying. Eating with fat or protein slows glucose absorption. Carisma's natural GI advantage compounds with smart cooking.
